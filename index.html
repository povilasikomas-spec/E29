Listikom

AI-powered listing assistant

How Listikom works

1. Open your Etsy listing

Start a new listing or edit an existing one in Etsy’s listing editor.

2. Click Generate in Listikom

Use the Listikom panel to generate a title, description, or tags from your product images.

3. Review and publish

Edit anything you want, then save your listing as usual. You stay in full control.

What Listikom helps you with

AI-generated titles

Create clear, relevant titles based on your product images, without starting from a blank box.

Detailed descriptions

Get readable, structured descriptions you can tweak before publishing your listing.

Relevant tags

Generate tag suggestions that match your product, making it easier for buyers to find your items.

Built for Etsy sellers

Listikom works directly inside Etsy’s own listing editor. It does not automatically publish or change listings on its own — you trigger every action. You can edit or ignore any AI suggestion before you save.

Privacy Policy

Listikom is a Chrome extension that runs in your browser on top of Etsy’s listing editor. When you use Listikom, the extension may send product images and listing text to our servers for the sole purpose of generating titles, descriptions, and tags.
